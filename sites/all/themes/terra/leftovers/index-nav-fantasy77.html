Razhan Mol's Story
  1. North America
  2. Africa

The son of the ruthless King Magnus Mol, Razhan has surpassed even his father's horrific reign. Razhan received nothing from his father except life and a command to take Tempest Hall, a citadel ruled by the powerful mage, Xiun. On the first full moon, Razhan Mol races to Tempest Hall to claim his legacy. He goes alone, with only his sword, Dark Sister, and his shield, Soul Eater, as his defense. Within one day and one night, the only survivors that remain are himself and Xiun the Crystal Mage who is trapped in the highest tower of Tempest Hall, awaiting her fate.

Razhan Mol is quick to create an army of goblins, led by two brothers, Glak the Ravager and Ganto the Cleaver. The army of goblins quickly grows and spreads, unleashing an era of darkness and destruction.
